I'm currently a PhD student in Mathematics at Università degli studi Roma Tre under the supervision of prof. Fabrizio Barroero.

My research focuses on Algebraic Number Theory and Diophantine geometry, in particular on problems of Unlikely Intersections and Chabauty methods for finding rational points on curves.

When I'm not thinking about math, I like to listen to classical music, play the violin, viola, and piano, and try to compose music.
